Nike, Inc. is a global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories, headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, USA. The company was founded on January 25, 1964, as Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman, a track and field coach at the University of Oregon, and his former student Phil Knight. It officially became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971. The name "Nike" comes from the Greek goddess of victory, symbolizing strength, speed, and triumph.

WebAbout

Nike is best known for its high-performance footwear, apparel, and equipment, tailored for a variety of sports, including basketball, soccer, running, and tennis. Its commitment to innovation is evident in breakthrough technologies like Nike Air, Flyknit, and React foam, which have revolutionized the design of athletic shoes. A key part of Nike’s success lies in its partnerships with world-class athletes. Its long-standing relationship with basketball legend Michael Jordan led to the creation of the Air Jordan line, which remains one of the company’s most successful and culturally influential product lines. Other top athletes like Serena Williams, Cristiano Ronaldo, and LeBron James have also contributed to Nike’s global appeal. In addition to performance, Nike places a growing emphasis on sustainability, incorporating recycled materials into its products and improving its environmental impact through initiatives like the Move to Zero campaign. Nike’s “Just Do It” slogan has transcended marketing to become a symbol of empowerment, motivating millions worldwide to push their limits and achieve greatness in sports and life.
